About me:

I am an assistant professor in the department of Mathematical and Statistical Sciences (MSSC) at Marquette University in Milwaukee, Wisconsin.

CV (Updated Jan. 2023)

Research interests:

Machine learning, optimization, and compressed sensing, with applications to image reconstruction in MRI, CT, and related inverse problems in computational imaging. My recent projects concern:

  • Solving inverse problems in medical imaging with physics-driven deep neural networks
  • Image quality assessment in CT with model observers
  • Mathematics of learning with neural networks in the overparameterized regime
